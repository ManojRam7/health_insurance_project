## 1. Context & Objective

The Gold `fact_members` layer transforms **clean Silver member records** into a **risk-segmented analytics-ready dimension** that:

- 👤 Creates a single, trusted member view  
- ✅ Adds actuarial risk segmentation  
- 📊 Enables health & claims prediction  
- 🎯 Powers customer targeting & pricing  
- 🔒 Maintains transparent data quality  

**Enterprise standards applied:**
- Risk segmentation (age, BMI, chronic disease)  
- Health indicators & employment grouping  
- Geographic segmentation  
- DQ flag carryover  
- Schema enforcement  
- Metrics logging  

---

## 2. Silver vs Gold fact_members

| Area | Silver Members | Gold fact_members |
|------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Core Attributes** | Raw demographics | Plus risk bands & segments |
| **Age** | Raw value (0–150) | Age_Band (18–29, 30–44, 45–59, 60+) |
| **BMI** | Raw value | BMI_Band (Underweight, Normal, Overweight, Obese) |
| **Health** | Raw chronic disease text | Chronic_Flag (0/1) + Chronic_Group (Diabetes, etc.) |
| **Smoking** | Smoker_Flag (0/1) | Smoker_Status (Smoker / Non-Smoker) |
| **Employment** | Raw text variants | Employment_Group (Employed, Student, Retired, Other) |
| **Geography** | Raw region code | Region_Group (Region 1–100, 101–200, etc.) |
| **DQ Flags** | dq_age_valid, dq_bmi_valid | Same flags inherited |
| **BI Readiness** | Intermediate | Full (segments pre-computed) |
| **Segmentation** | Limited | Rich (risk-based) |

**Result:** Risk-segmented member dimension trusted for actuarial & ML.

## 3. Transformations: Silver → Gold

### 3.1 Read & Enforce Schema

Cast member columns to enterprise schema:
```
Member_ID, Policy_ID, First_Name, Last_Name,
Age, Gender, BMI, Smoker_Flag,
Chronic_Disease, Employment_Status, Region,
dq_age_valid, dq_bmi_valid
```

### 3.2 Age Segmentation

Bucket into 4 actuary-friendly bands:
```python
Age_Band = CASE
  WHEN Age < 30 THEN "18–29"
  WHEN Age < 45 THEN "30–44"
  WHEN Age < 60 THEN "45–59"
  ELSE "60+"
END
```

**Business value:**
- 📊 Age-based pricing tiers  
- 💰 Risk stratification  
- 🏥 Health claims correlation  

### 3.3 BMI Segmentation

Classify into WHO-standard bands:
```python
BMI_Band = CASE
  WHEN BMI < 18.5 THEN "Underweight"
  WHEN BMI < 25 THEN "Normal"
  WHEN BMI < 30 THEN "Overweight"
  ELSE "Obese"
END
```

**Business value:**
- 🚨 Health risk indicator  
- 📈 Claims frequency predictor  
- 🏥 Actuarial pricing input  

### 3.4 Smoker Status Labeling

Convert flag to human-readable status:
```python
Smoker_Status = CASE
  WHEN Smoker_Flag = 1 THEN "Smoker"
  ELSE "Non-Smoker"
END
```

**Business value:**
- 💼 Risk segmentation  
- 💰 Premium differentiation  
- 🔍 Health behavior indicator  

### 3.5 Chronic Disease Grouping

Create two fields: flag + category:
```python
Chronic_Flag = CASE WHEN Chronic_Disease IS NOT NULL THEN 1 ELSE 0 END

Chronic_Group = CASE
  WHEN Chronic_Disease LIKE '%Diabetes%' THEN "Diabetes"
  WHEN Chronic_Disease LIKE '%Hypertension%' THEN "Hypertension"
  WHEN Chronic_Disease LIKE '%Asthma%' THEN "Asthma"
  ELSE "Other"
END
```

**Business value:**
- 🏥 Disease-specific risk scoring  
- 📈 Claims prediction  
- 🎯 Targeted health programs  

### 3.6 Employment & Region Grouping

Standardize employment & geography:
```python
Employment_Group = CASE
  WHEN Employment_Status = 'Employed' THEN "Employed"
  WHEN Employment_Status = 'Student' THEN "Student"
  WHEN Employment_Status = 'Retired' THEN "Retired"
  ELSE "Other"
END

Region_Group = CASE
  WHEN Region BETWEEN 1 AND 100 THEN "Region 1–100"
  WHEN Region BETWEEN 101 AND 200 THEN "Region 101–200"
  ELSE "Region 200+"
END
```

**Business value:**
- 📍 Geographic risk variation  
- 💼 Socioeconomic segmentation  
- 🎯 Regional targeting  

### 3.7 DQ Flag Carryover

Preserve Silver DQ signals:
- `dq_age_valid` (age plausibility: 0–150)  
- `dq_bmi_valid` (BMI plausibility: 10–60)  

**Business value:**
- 🔍 Dashboard filtering capability  
- 📋 Quality transparency  
- ✅ Governance  

### 3.8 Write to Gold

- **Delta path:** `silverdata/gold/fact_members`  
- **Table:** `bupa_gold.fact_members`  
- **Schema-aware overwrite** ensures stability

## 5. Risk Segmentation Strategy

### Why Segmentation Matters

Insurance is fundamentally about **risk assessment**. Gold member segmentation enables:

| Use Case | Segments Used |
|----------|---------------|
| **Pricing & Underwriting** | Age, BMI, Smoker, Chronic, Region |
| **Churn Prediction** | Age, Employment, Region |
| **Claims Modeling** | Age, BMI, Smoker, Chronic, Region |
| **Customer Targeting** | Age, Employment, Chronic, Region |
| **Health Programs** | Chronic_Group, Age, BMI |

### Segmentation Hierarchy

```
Member
  ├─ Demographics (Age_Band, Gender)
  ├─ Health (BMI_Band, Smoker_Status, Chronic_Flag, Chronic_Group)
  ├─ Socioeconomic (Employment_Group)
  └─ Geographic (Region_Group)
```

Each dimension powers different analytical use cases.

### Age Band Risk Profile

| Band | Risk Level | Claims Pattern | Typical Actions |
|------|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **18–29** | Low | Preventive care | Wellness programs |
| **30–44** | Low-Medium | Acute events | Accident prevention |
| **45–59** | Medium | Chronic onset | Health screening |
| **60+** | High | Multiple chronic | Specialized coverage |

### BMI Risk Profile

| Band | Risk Level | Health Condition | Policy Impact |
|------|-----------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Underweight** | Low | Malnutrition risk | Standard rates |
| **Normal** | Low | Optimal | Standard rates |
| **Overweight** | Medium | Metabolic risk | +10–15% premium |
| **Obese** | High | Multiple conditions | +20–30% premium |

### Chronic Disease Impact

| Disease Group | Prevalence | Claims Cost | Intervention |
|---------------|-----------|------------|--------------|
| **Diabetes** | High | Very High | Monitoring program |
| **Hypertension** | High | High | Medication adherence |
| **Asthma** | Medium | Medium | Preventive inhalers |
| **Other** | Variable | Variable | Case-by-case |
