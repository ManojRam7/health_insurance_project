1. PURPOSE OF GOLD FACT MEMBERS
====================================================================

The Gold fact_members table is the **final, business-consumable** dataset
representing enriched and segmented information about every insured member.

It is used directly by:

• BI dashboards  
• Pricing teams  
• Actuarial & risk modeling  
• Customer analytics  
• Retention teams  
• Claims severity / frequency models  

GOLD = “fully prepared analytical dataset”  
SILVER = “clean, validated operational truth”

Gold enhances Silver by adding:

• Rich segmentation (BMI bands, Age bands, Region groups, Employment groups)  
• Risk-sensitive flags (Chronic disease flag, Smoker status)  
• Derived business KPIs (used in pricing & underwriting models)  
• DQ indicators (dq_age_valid, dq_bmi_valid)  
• Human-readable labels for dashboards  
• Fully flattened fact table with no joins required by BI

2. SILVER → GOLD TRANSFORMATION LOGIC
====================================================================

THE GOLD LAYER ADDS:

--------------------------------------------------------
A) Customer Risk Segmentation
--------------------------------------------------------
1. Age_Band  
   - “18–29”, “30–44”, “45–59”, “60+”  
   - Used in underwriting, cross-sell segmentation, actuarial pricing.

2. BMI_Band  
   - “Normal”, “Overweight”, “Obese”, etc.  
   - Business value: Predictive of claims cost & chronic conditions.

3. Smoker_Status  
   - Converted from Y/N to “Smoker” / “Non-Smoker” labels  
   - Used in risk scoring.

4. Chronic_Flag & Chronic_Group  
   - Groups chronic diseases consistently  
   - Allows for better risk forecasting.

5. Employment_Group  
   - “Employed”, “Student”, “Retired”, “Unemployed”  
   - Helps segmentation for marketing & pricing.

6. Region_Group  
   - Converts raw Region code → ranges (“Region_100–149”)  
   - Used for geographic analysis and pricing variations.

--------------------------------------------------------
B) Data Quality Indicators
--------------------------------------------------------
dq_age_valid  
dq_bmi_valid  

These flags indicate whether critical numeric fields are valid.  
Instead of removing bad data, we allow analysts to filter or include it
based on business context.

--------------------------------------------------------
C) Final Flattened Table for BI  
--------------------------------------------------------
Gold fact_members contains:

Member_ID  
Policy_ID  
Age  
Gender  
BMI  
Smoker_Flag  
Chronic_Disease  
Employment_Status  
Region  
(Derived Segments)  
(DQ Flags)  

Total columns: ~18 (depending on segmentation logic)

No joins. No transformations needed. Ready for dashboards & ML.

5. INTERVIEW SCRIPT (READY TO SPEAK)
====================================================================

“I built the Gold fact_members dataset to serve as the primary analytical
foundation for understanding customer demographics, behaviour, and health
risk. The Silver layer ensures the data is clean and validated; Gold transforms

In Gold, I applied segmentation logic—Age bands, BMI categories, smoker/non-smoker 
status, chronic condition grouping, employment segmentation, and region grouping.

can use it directly without additional joins or transformations.”
